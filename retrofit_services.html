RETROFIT & REPLACEMENT

Ommax Electric professionally competent and experienced engineers provide a Retrofit and Replacement Service for Circuit Breakers (incl. ABB, Siemens, Schneider, Eaton & reputed brands), Low/Medium Voltage Switchgear panels, Control Panels, Metering panels and Mains/Generator Power Transfer Systems.

RETROFIT is the upgrading/ modernisation of critical components in existing switchgear systems with the aim to improve their overall technical, safety-related and economical properties. In these systems, the components subject to the highest level of wear and tear are the circuit breakers and disconnectors. Medium - and low voltage circuit breakers RETROFIT is the most cost-effective method to modernize and extend the service life of existing switchgear.

1

Conversions

Personnel and equipment protection is improved by for ie. installing modern protection technology in the existing systems, fixed construction to draw-out construction by utilizing the existing panels.

2

Replacements

Replacement of for example older technology circuit breakers with modern microprocessor based circuit breakers, leaving the existing switchgear unchanged.

3

Extensions

Extensions include equipping spare switchgear panels or extending existing switchgear with additional panels of the same or of a more modern design.

4

Modernisation

Modernisation of other system components according to the latest status of technology, thus improving system availability and uprating its operating ratings.

Your benefits from RETROFIT

  • Reduction of maintenance costs.
  • Completely new customized with latest technology.
  • No tedious authorisation processes.
  • Improvement of environmental protection.
  • Inventory protection because the existing systems retain their structure.
  • Significantly lower investment costs.
  • Improvement of system and personal protection.
  • Extension of the switchgear system life cycle.
  • Short installation times and minimal disruption.
  • No need for re-cabling or civil works.
