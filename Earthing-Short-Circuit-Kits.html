Earthing & Short Circuit Kits

We offer a wide range of Earthing & Short-Circuiting Kits in the shape of portable earthing kits and customized earthing kits, which are specially designed to meet the exact requirements of our clients.

The equipment is designed to prevent shock from returning voltages.

1

Earthing and Short-Circuit kit:

◆ Earthing and short-circuit kit is to be used on medium voltage overhead lines and the clamp is secured in place by pressure and can be removed by the operating hook. Three clamps can be locked in place simultaneously.
◆ The kit complies with IEC 61230 standard
◆ 3 x MC aluminum alloy clamps with automatic activation fastening for cylindrical conductors with a diameter ranging from 3 to 20 mm
◆ 1 x Dispenser head with hook and M-10 thread
◆ 2 x Copper cables with PVC insulation (16 / 25 / 35 mm2 section – depending on model) – 2.5m long
◆ 1 x PVC Insulated Copper Cable (16 / 25 mm2 section – depending on model); length: 15m
◆ 1 x Metallic coil to store the 15 m earthing cable
◆ 1 x Telescopic Pole (1.1 m when folded and 2 m when extended) with anchorage button
◆ 1 x Earthing Rod
◆ 1 x Plastic Carry Case
◆ 1 x Rods Storage Bag

2

Earthing Single Earthing Leads:

◆ Single Earthing Leads are single-phase earthing equipment. Single Earthing Leads have a B or H clamp which allows the earthing connection of the lines for repair operations.
◆ 1 x Aluminum alloy clamp closing by screw and bayonet fixing for cylindrical conductors with diameter between 4 mm and 30 mm. Glove Storage Box
◆ 1 x Copper cable with PVC insulation of 50 mm2 section and 7 m long
◆ 1 Carrying Bag to store and transport the equipment
