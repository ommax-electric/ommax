Insulated Matting

We offer a wide range of Insulation mattes which are specially designed to meet the IEC 61111 safety requirements of our clients.

Insulating matting are also known as dielectric carpets, and are used to protect people and parts of electrical equipment used in switchboards, transformers and other high voltage workplaces.

1

CLASS 3 Insulating Matting: 

◆ Insulated matting marked in conformity with the IEC 61111 requirements
◆ Working voltage: 26kV
◆ Withstand voltage: 40kV
◆ Matting is packed in rolls to 10 meters standard length or can be cut into sheets
◆ Class: 3
◆ Colour: Grey
◆ Hardness: 65 ShA
◆ Surface: Smooth
◆ Thickness: 3mm
◆ Strength: 6 MPa
◆ Basic weight: 4.6 kg/m2

2

CLASS 4 Insulating Matting:

◆ Insulated matting marked in conformity with the IEC 61111 requirements.
◆ Working voltage: 36kV
◆ Withstand voltage: 50kV
◆ Matting is packed in rolls to 10 meters standard length or can be cut into sheets
◆ Class: 4
◆ Colour: Grey
◆ Hardness: 65 ShA
◆ Surface: Smooth
◆ Thickness: 4 mm
◆ Strength: 6 MPa
◆ Basic weight: 6.1 kg/m2

3

Insulating Matting Bag:

◆ The Insulating Matting Bag is specially designed for carrying and protecting insulating mats. The Insulating Matting Bag is equipped with a shoulder strap and a plastic window for instructions and storage identification.
