Earthing Clamps & Fittings

We offer a wide range of Earthing clamps & fittings, which are specially designed to meet the exact requirements of our clients.

Earthing clamps are used to connect earth electrodes (earth rods) with conductors.

1

Rod to Tape Clamp ( Type A ):

◆ Earthing clamps are used to connect earth electrodes (earth rods) with conductors or tapes. A Type Clamps are one type of earthing clamp, used to join earth rods and copper tapes. The earth rod and tape passes through the clamp body and a screw fastener secures both together.
◆ The clamps are made of gunmetal or brass.
◆ Abrasion resistance
◆ Sturdy construction
◆ Fine finish

Available with different rod and cable sizes

SIZE MATERIAL
26 x 12mm Gunmetal / Brass
31 x 12mm Gunmetal / Brass
40 x 10mm Gunmetal / Brass
51 x 8mm Gunmetal / Brass
2

Rod to Cable Clamp ( Type G ):

◆ Earthing clamps are used to connect earth electrodes (earth rods) with conductors or tapes. G Type Clamps are one type of earthing clamp, used to join earth rods and copper circular conductors. The earth rod and conductor passes through the clamp body and a screw fastener secures both together.
◆ G clamps are made of gunmetal or brass.
◆ Abrasion resistance
