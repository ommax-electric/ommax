Life Saving Kits

We offer a wide range of rescue kits, which are specially designed to meet the exact requirements of our clients.

The rescue kits specifically designed for M.V substations and typical contains products such as insulating sticks, voltage detectors, rescue hooks, insulating gloves & boots, and a safety poster.

1

Medium Voltage Electrical Rescue Kit: 

◆ We design and supply customized wall-mounted or cabinet-style MV Electrical Rescue Kits. These kits can be tailored at the time of order to your specific requirements and can include your company logo if necessary. All boards include a ‘shadow board effect’ indicating the location of each safety device. All components are supplied to relevant IEC Standards.
◆ CPR Sign
◆ Glove Storage Box
◆ Class 3 Dielectric Gloves
◆ 45kV Insulated Stool
◆ Analogic MV Detector with Operating Pole
◆ Rescue Pole 1.75 m 45kV and brackets

2

LV Low Voltage Electrical Rescue Kit:

◆ We design and supply customized wall-mounted or cabinet-style LV Electrical Rescue Kits. These kits can be tailored at the time of order to your specific requirements and can include your company logo if necessary. All boards include a ‘shadow board effect’ indicating the location of each safety device. All components are supplied to relevant IEC Standards.
◆ CPR Sign
◆ Glove Storage Box
◆ Class 0 Dielectric Gloves
◆ Phase Check LV Detector
◆ Class 3 Insulating Matting – working voltage up to 26kV
◆ Rescue Pole 1.75 m 45kV and brackets

3

Portable Life Saving Kit:
