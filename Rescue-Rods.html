Rescue Rods

We offer a wide range of Rescue rods and rescue poles, which are specially designed to meet the exact requirements of our clients.

The Rescue rods are used to remove the casualty from the danger area in rescue operations.

1

Safety Rescue Hook: 

◆ This Rescue Hook is designed to pull out persons from the source of danger. Made out of composite materials the rod is not only durable and strong but also fully non-conductive. Suitable for both indoor and outdoor use (but only in dry conditions)
◆ Maximum Voltage: 33 kV
◆ Test Voltage: 60 kV
◆ Mechanical Strength: 500N for the duration of 1min.
◆ Weight: 1kg

2

Rescue Pole 1.75m 45kV :

◆ Rescue Pole is a rescue rod with only one section, made of polyester and fiberglass tube with 32 mm diameter. Rescue Pole is used to remove the casualty from the danger area in rescue operations. Rescue Pole boasts a total length of 1.75 meters and a working voltage of 45kV
◆ Humidity-proof seals on each end
◆ Hand Protector
◆ Grip
◆ M-10 Metric Head
◆ Rescue Hook
◆ Total Length of 1.75 meters
◆ Working Voltage: 45 kV
