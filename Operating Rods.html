Operating Rods

We offer a wide range of Rescue rods, which are specially designed to meet the exact requirements of our clients.

The Rescue rods are used to remove the casualty from the danger area in rescue operations. 

1

Multi Use Poles:

◆ Designed for multi use – these connectable poles are made to comply with IEC 60855 and UNE-EN-50508 European standards. Each pole is composed of two or three connectable sections each fabricated of very strong polyurethane fiberglass tube that offers improved durability
◆ Head types: U – Universal / B – Bayonet / H – Polyvalent
◆ For nominal voltages up to 60 kV
◆ Not to be used with precipitation.

2

Telescopic Insulation Pole:

◆ Telescopic Insulating Pole is an operating rod with two telescopic sections, made of reinforced fiberglass tube (40mm and 32 mm in diameter). Telescopic Insulating Pole features an adjustment system with locking handles and humidity-proof seals on each end, hand protector, grip, tip and head. Humidity-proof seals on each end
◆ Length closed: 1,15 meters
◆ Length extended: 2 meters
◆ Working voltage: 66 kV full extended
◆ For INDOOR and OUTDOOR use in dry environment
