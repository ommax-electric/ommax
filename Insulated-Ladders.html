Insulated Ladders

We offer a wide range of Insulated Ladders, which are specially designed to meet the exact requirements of our clients.

We supply both single and double rung insulating stepladders that comply with EN 131 safety standards.

1

Double Rung Extendable Ladder: 

◆ For maximum versatility, you should choose the Double Rung Extendable Ladder that offers an excellent combination of double rung ladder and extendable ladder. The ladder is made of strong and rugged fiberglass and aluminum and can withstand voltage spikes of up to 30,000 volts. EN 131 compliant.

2

Extendable/Dual Rung Ladder:

◆ The Extendable/Dual Rung Ladder offers significant advantages over competitors. It can be used as a dual rung ladder or single rung extendable ladder. The use of fiberglass and aluminum ensures that the ladder is shockproof up to 30,000 volts. EN 131 compliant.

3

Standard Insulated Ladder:

◆ The Standard Insulated Ladders range is made of dielectric fiberglass and aluminum to meet EN 131 safety standards. Protects against the accidental shock of up to 30,000 V.

4

Insulating Stepladder:

◆ Insulating Stepladder offers insulation between two steps of 30,000 V (test performed after immersion in water for 24 hours). Insulating Stepladder features high mechanical resistance to bending/twisting and high fire resistance.
